This is a pet app where you can feed and play with your own virtual animals.

Creative portion - shop a few of yummy nummy stuffs for your pets!

The “shop” button at the bottom right corner of the screen will lead you to a new page for shopping. In a list of available items, you can click “buy” to make a purchase or “redeem” to obtain more coins. The refined food will provide your pets with more energy and joy when you go back and use it on the main screen.

When a pet reaches maximum food level and happiness, a unicorn gallops over a rainbow and the sky rains lollipops and gummy bears - not really what’s happening, but you do get rewards for taking good care of your pet! You can evolve it to a Pusheen who changes gestures every time you feed it with a different type of food.
